About MadX

Welcome to MadX Clothing, the final capstone project for Madhav Agarwal. This website is a demonstration of frontend development skills using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, built for the SNW subject at Newton School of Technology.

Our mentor is Mohd. Zakir sir. This project focuses on building a dynamic and responsive e-commerce experience from the ground up.

MadX is built on the idea that your jacket is more than just clothing—it's a statement. We (hypothetically) source the best materials to create durable, stylish outerwear for the modern rebel.
