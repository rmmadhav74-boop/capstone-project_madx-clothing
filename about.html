About MadX

MadX Clothing is a project dedicated to crafting jackets for those who move against the current. Built with a modern, dark aesthetic and engineered details, every piece is designed to feel confident in the city and resilient in the weather.

Phase 1 delivers a clean, responsive frontend experience. Phase 2 will introduce a robust backend to power product data, cart features, and order flows. Until then, explore our collection and imagine your edge.

Thank you for visiting and supporting the journey. Stay bold.
